Experience

Focusing on trademark law since 1994, Ms. Ryan’s practice encompasses trademark, trade dress, unfair competition, and Internet-related issues including international trademark filing and prosecution, policing and enforcement, oppositions and cancellations in foreign jurisdictions and before the USPTO Trademark Trial and Appeal Board, trademark selection, clearance opinions, and issues concerning trademark maintenance. Ms. Ryan also advises on trademark issues related to licensing, acquisitions and due diligence, and the specific trademark needs of franchisors.

Ms. Ryan’s experience includes major company name changes and international brand launches, and she regularly helps clients assess and enhance their trademark portfolios and procedures to meet changing business objectives. She represents leading companies in the hospitality, insurance and financial, medical products, medical services, retail, and industrial products industries.
