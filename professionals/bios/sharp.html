Experience

Mr. Sharp earned his degree from the University of Notre Dame in mechanical engineering. While at Notre Dame, he spent time researching high speed machining. After leaving Notre Dame, Mr. Sharp received training as a software engineer at the Infosys Technologies, Ltd. Global Education Center in Mysore, India. Mr. Sharp continued his work as a Software Engineer for Infosys Technologies prior to attending law school at William Mitchell College of Law. Following law school, Mr. Sharp joined the IBM Patent Center, where he prepared and prosecuted patents for IBM in the electrical, mechanical, and software arts.
